Roasting

Roasting is a cooking method that uses dry heat where hot air covers the food, cooking it evenly on all sides.

Sauteing

Sauteing is a methos of cooking that uses a relatively small amount of oil or fat in a shallow pan over relatively high heat.

Grilling

Grilling involves cooking food on a rack over a heat source, usually a charcoal fire or ceramic briquettes heated by gas flames.

Boiling

Boiling is a moist heat cooking method that happens when the liquid's temperature reaches 212 degrees.

Steaming

Steaming is a cooking method that is created when boiling water vaporizes into steam and makes contact with food.
